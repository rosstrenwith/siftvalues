Values are the words we use to articulate and celebrate what makes Sift, well... Sift. Our Culture is actually putting those words into action, everyday.

Put People First

We are a bunch of creative, kind and skilled people that encourage each other to be better today than we were yesterday; in the pursuit of doing what we love, in the best way we can.

Be Curious

We are trusted and supported to try new and exciting things which make our jobs more enjoyable. We don’t want to do things the way they’ve always been done. We want to ask questions and challenge ideas to seek better understanding and do a better job.

Inspire Action

Our best work happens when great people come together. Everyone can make a change, we embrace change. We love new ideas, we strive for quality and together we make things happen.

Be Honest

We trust each other. We create an environment in which we feel confident to speak openly and listen. We welcome feedback that pushes us to new heights. We treat others how we would like to be treated.

Stay Focused

Our passion and individual sense of purpose allows us to set our sights on what we want to achieve. We take ownership of what we want, set ourselves a clear path, see things through, and measure how we did.

Have Fun

A good days work isn’t always measured with metrics, sometimes it’s in the warm fuzzy feeling we get from working with people we like.
